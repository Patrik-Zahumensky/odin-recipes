Reuben Sandwich

A Reuben sandwich is a delicious and savory grilled sandwich made with corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Russian or Thousand Island dressing on rye bread. It's a quick and easy meal that pairs well with a pickle, potato chips, French fries, or classic deli sides like pasta salad, coleslaw, and tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • Â½ cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a large griddle or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread one side of bread slices evenly with Thousand Island dressing.
  3. On four bread slices, layer one slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut, and a second slice of Swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, dressing-side down. Butter the top of each sandwich.
  4. Place sandwiches, butter-side down on the preheated griddle; butter the top of each sandwich with remaining butter. Grill until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot with your choice of side dish.
